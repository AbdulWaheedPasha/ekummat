Ekummat.com

We are a non-profit organization using the digital media platform to help spread the message of Allah to the common man through simple roman English. Our internal survey indicates that people have difficulty understanding and reading Urdu or English Tafseer. We have started this initiative so that a beginner or basic reader who has some English skills can now easily read and understand the Tafseer.

Thankfully, after months of hard work, we've been able to finish this project. It is important to note that the amount of the content to be used or shared in relation as a whole, such as using a Hadith in its entirety, or using one chapter from a Surah. Manipulating, cutting or cropping and/or pasting, attaching with other part of this website wherein it loses its authenticity and understanding is not allowed Throughout the content on this website, expert guidance has been provided and the utmost care has been taken to eliminate any mistakes. You are welcome to notify us if you find any errors.
